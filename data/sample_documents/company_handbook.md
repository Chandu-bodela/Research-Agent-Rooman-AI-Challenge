# Northwind Traders — Employee Handbook (2026 Edition)

## 1. Leave Policy

Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days of paid time off (PTO) for every completed month of
service, up to a maximum of 18 days per calendar year. Unused PTO can be carried over into
the next year, capped at 5 days. Employees must submit leave requests through the HR portal
at least 5 business days in advance for non-emergency leave.

Sick leave is separate from PTO: employees receive 10 paid sick days per year, which do not
roll over. A doctor's note is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## 2. Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Fully remote
arrangements require VP-level sign-off and are reviewed quarterly. All remote employees must
be reachable during core hours, 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time, and must attend the monthly
in-person town hall unless on approved leave.

## 3. Expense Reimbursement

Employees are reimbursed for reasonable business expenses, including client meals (up to
$75 per person), local transportation, and approved software subscriptions. Expense reports
must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date via the Finance portal, with itemized
receipts attached. Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days of approval.

## 4. Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice a year: a mid-year check-in in June and a full annual
review in December. Ratings use a 5-point scale (1 = Needs Improvement, 5 = Outstanding).
Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for the annual merit increase pool, which is typically
between 3% and 6% of base salary depending on company performance.

## 5. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect. Harassment,
discrimination, or retaliation of any kind will not be tolerated and should be reported to HR
or via the anonymous ethics hotline. Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and
including termination.

## 6. Equipment Policy

New employees receive a company laptop and a one-time $300 home-office stipend for desk,
chair, and monitor setup. Equipment must be returned within 14 days of the employee's last
day, in working condition, barring normal wear and tear.
