Northwind Traders — Q1 2026 Market Research Summary

Overview
This report summarizes findings from our Q1 2026 customer survey (n=1,240 respondents) and
competitive analysis of three main competitors: Alpine Retail, Coastal Supply Co., and
Meridian Goods.

Customer Satisfaction
Overall customer satisfaction (CSAT) rose to 82%, up from 76% in Q4 2025. The improvement is
largely attributed to the new same-day delivery option launched in January 2026, which 68% of
surveyed customers said they had used at least once. Net Promoter Score (NPS) improved from
+24 to +31 over the same period.

Pricing Sensitivity
41% of respondents said price was their top purchasing factor, followed by delivery speed
(27%) and product quality (19%). Customers in the 18-29 age bracket were significantly more
price-sensitive than those aged 45+, with 58% citing price as the top factor versus 29%.

Competitive Landscape
Alpine Retail continues to lead on price, averaging 8% lower prices across our tracked basket
of 50 SKUs. However, Alpine's average delivery time (4.2 days) is nearly double ours (2.3 days).
Coastal Supply Co. has the highest customer satisfaction among competitors at 79% CSAT, driven
by their loyalty program, which we do not currently have an equivalent to. Meridian Goods has
seen declining market share, dropping from 18% to 14% of the regional market over the past year.

Recommendations
1. Launch a loyalty/rewards program in Q2 2026 to close the gap with Coastal Supply Co.
2. Consider targeted discounting for the 18-29 segment, where price sensitivity is highest.
3. Continue investing in delivery speed as a key differentiator versus Alpine Retail.
4. Monitor Meridian Goods' pricing moves closely, as their declining share may lead to
   aggressive discounting in Q2.

Methodology Note
Survey was conducted online between January 15 and February 10, 2026, with a margin of error
of +/- 2.8% at a 95% confidence level. Competitive pricing data was collected via weekly
automated price checks across each competitor's top 50 SKUs by volume.
